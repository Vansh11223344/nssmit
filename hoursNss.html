Attendence

Within the NSS MAHE MIT Unit 1 and Unit 2, a dedicated group of passionate volunteers stands as the cornerstone of our vibrant community. Guided by the wisdom of our Programme Officers and Unit Heads, these two units collectively form an unwavering force for good in society. United by a shared commitment to service, each volunteer plays an essential role in bringing our initiatives to life. With diverse backgrounds and unique talents, they continuously demonstrate the power of selfless dedication, working together to create lasting positive change. Their commitment, energy, and enthusiasm serve as an inspiration to all, showcasing the true spirit of the National Service Scheme.
